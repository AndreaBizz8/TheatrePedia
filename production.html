Plot

Based on the film of the same name, School of Rock—The Musical tells the story of wannabe rock star Dewey Finn, who poses as a substitute teacher at a prestigious prep school, while impersonating his best friend, Ned Schneebly. When he discovers his students are musical talents, he enlists his fifth-graders to form a rock group and conquer the Battle of the Bands, in order to achieve his own dream of being a famous rock star.
